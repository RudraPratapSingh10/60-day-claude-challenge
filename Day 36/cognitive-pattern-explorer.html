Cognitive Pattern Explorer

Wander through a handful of everyday moments and notice how you tend to think through them. There's nothing to score and nothing to get right — only patterns worth noticing.

This experience is educational and reflective only. It does not diagnose, measure, or clinically assess mental health, personality, or cognitive ability — it simply offers a mirror for noticing your own habits.
