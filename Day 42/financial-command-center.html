Money Tips for Students

01Use the 50/30/20 idea loosely: needs, wants, savings — even $10/week toward savings builds the habit.
02Track every income source separately. Irregular gig income is easier to budget when averaged over 3 months.
03Before subscribing to anything, add it to your budget first — see if it actually fits.
04Build a small emergency fund before anything else. Even $200 prevents most "emergency" debt.
05Student discounts exist almost everywhere — software, transit, food. Always ask.
06Review this dashboard weekly, not daily. Weekly check-ins build consistency without burnout.
