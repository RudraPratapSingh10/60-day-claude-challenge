Wrap-up

Workflow Summary

Building a developer portfolio end-to-end runs through eight linked stages: define your positioning and sitemap, design the visual system, write the content that proves your work, build the frontend, wire up backend pieces like the contact form, test for quality and accessibility, deploy to a live domain, and then keep it growing with SEO and periodic updates. AI tools do the most good in the writing-heavy stages (Discovery, Content) and the code-heavy stages (Frontend, Backend), where they compress hours of drafting or boilerplate into minutes — but the judgment calls (positioning, visual taste, what to launch with) stay yours.
