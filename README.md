## 🧠 What is this?

This is my **60-day public commitment** to go from someone who *uses* AI to someone who *builds* with it.

Every day for 60 days, I will:
- 🔨 Build something real with Claude AI
- 📖 Document what I learned
- 💡 Share prompts, projects, and breakthroughs
- ❌ Share failures too — no highlight reel

> *"Stop collecting ideas. Start shipping artifacts."*
> — My Claude AI Personality Profile
